What PlanSwipe is

One shared way to decide where to go

Create a group with your friends, agree on the basics, swipe through real options, and watch the front-runners rise to the top. No polls, no pressure, and no endless thread that never reaches a decision.

Agree on the basics first

Everyone chooses an area and the type of night out together. Once the group agrees, PlanSwipe takes it from there — no endless back-and-forth.

Discover real places

Get live suggestions from Google Maps matched to your group's area and activity, with photos, ratings, hours and reviews — not generic ideas.

Vote and match together

Swipe through each spot — right to like, left to pass — and instantly see which places your group agrees on. When everyone's in, you've got your plan.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

What is PlanSwipe?

PlanSwipe is a group planning app that helps friends decide where to go out. You pick an area and an activity together, swipe through real nearby places pulled from Google Maps, and vote to find the spot everyone agrees on.

How does the voting work?

Each member swipes right to like a place or left to pass. PlanSwipe combines everyone's votes and surfaces the places your whole group is into, so you reach a shared decision without a long group chat.

Is PlanSwipe free?

Yes — creating groups, discovering places, and voting together are free. An optional Pro plan is available for €1.99/month.

How do friends join my group?

Every group has a code and a shareable invite link. Send it to your friends and they join instantly.

Where do the place suggestions come from?

They're real venues from Google Maps, matched to your group's chosen area and activity, with photos, ratings, opening hours and reviews. You can add notes so suggestions fit what you're after.

Can we set a date once we agree on a place?

Yes. Once your group matches, you can lock in a date and time, RSVP so everyone sees who's coming, add it to your calendar, and get a reminder before the outing.

What if the group can't agree?

If the top places tie, PlanSwipe offers a quick runoff round so the group can settle on a single winner.

Does PlanSwipe work in Athens?

Yes — it's built for going out in Athens and the surrounding areas, with suggestions across neighbourhoods and the coast.
